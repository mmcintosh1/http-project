Welcome

Hypertext Transfer Protocol (HTTP) is the foundation of communication on the World Wide Web. Every time you visit a website, your browser uses HTTP to request information from a web server. This project explores the history of HTTP, how it works, its different versions, and why HTTPS is important for secure communication.
